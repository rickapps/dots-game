How to Play Dots

Players take turns drawing one horizontal or vertical line that connects two adjacent dots. Diagonal lines are not allowed. A player is awarded one point each time they draw the fourth side of a single square that completes a box. That player also continues drawing lines and gaining points provided each new line they draw completes another box. Remember, each time you complete a box, you must draw another line. Your turn ends and your opponent's turn starts when you draw a line that does not complete a box. The game is over when there are no more dots to connect. The player that completes the most boxes wins. Play against the computer, or against another person. Your game is saved to your computer. You can return to this website anytime to resume your last game in progress.
