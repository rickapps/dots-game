How to Play Dots

Objective:

The player that completes the most squares wins the game.

Instructions:

Players take turns drawing horizontal or vertical lines that connect two adjacent dots. If a player draws a line that completes a square of four adjacent dots, the player claims the square. But, the player must also draw an additional line to finish their turn. A player's turn ends only when the player draws a line that does not complete a square.

The game is over when all squares are claimed. The player with the most squares wins.

Play against the computer, or against another person. Your game is saved to your computer so you can return to this website anytime to finish a game in progress.
