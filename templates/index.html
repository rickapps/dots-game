How to Play:

Players take turns drawing one horizontal or vertical line that connects two adjacent dots. Diagonal lines are not allowed. A player is awarded one point each time they draw the fourth side of a 1x1 square that completes a box. That player also continues drawing lines and gaining points provided each new line they draw completes another box. Remember, each time you complete a box, you must draw another line. If you draw a line that does not complete a box, your turn ends, and your opponent's turn starts. The game is over when there are no more dots to connect. The player that completes the most boxes wins. Play against the computer, or with another person. Your game will be saved to your computer. Return to this web page anytime to resume a game in progress.
